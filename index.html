Step 03 · Roadmap

Your 5-year growth map

A phase-by-phase blueprint for the AI/ML Engineer track.

  1. Months 0 – 3

    Foundation Sprint

    Lock the basics. Linear algebra, probability, Python mastery. Ship 2 small ML projects on GitHub.

    • Andrew Ng ML Specialisation
    • Build a recommender system
    • Daily 30-min Leetcode warmups
  2. Months 3 – 9

    Deep Dive: Modern ML

    PyTorch, transformers, fine-tuning. Build 1 production-grade LLM-powered side project.

    • FastAI + Karpathy zero-to-hero
    • Fine-tune an open-source LLM
    • Write 3 blog posts on findings
  3. Year 1 – 2

    First ML Role

    Land an ML/AI Engineer role. Focus on shipping, not perfection. Build internal trust.

    • Apply to 50+ targeted teams
    • Own at least 1 model in production
    • Start mentoring interns
  4. Year 2 – 4

    Senior & Specialisation

    Pick a niche: LLM infra, agents, or retrieval. Become the go-to person on your team.

    • Lead a cross-team ML project
    • Publish or speak at a conference
    • Build a personal brand (newsletter)
  5. Year 4 – 5

    Lead the Team

    Move into tech-lead or staff. Influence roadmaps. Ship the developer tool you've been dreaming of.

    • Hire your first 2 reports
    • Launch the tool publicly
    • Reach 10k+ active users
